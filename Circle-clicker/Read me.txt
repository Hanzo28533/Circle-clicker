🎯 Circle Clicker – 2 Player HTML Game
A fun and fast-paced two-player browser game where you click on popping circles to earn points! Hit the circle to score, miss and lose a point. Compete in two back-to-back rounds and see who comes out on top!

🔹 Features:
🔔 Sound effect on successful clicks

❌ Penalty for missed clicks

👥 2-player turn-based gameplay

⏱️ 30-second rounds per player

🎨 Colorful, animated targets

💻 Playable in any modern browser (desktop or mobile)

▶️ How to Play
Open index.html in a browser

Click Start Game

Player 1 plays for 30 seconds

Player 2 plays right after

The winner is announced!
